Our idea


Inspite of regular repair of old building the temple building is leaking because of monkey's and some natural reasons. During rainy season the building is leaking and the devotees are facing problem especially the ladies are facing more problems. For this reason it was decided to repair the temple building since long. Now the time has come to repair the temple building. The work will be done without disturbing the basic structure and garbha gudi. For this the permission and Prasad was obtained. The repair work is as follows.



1. Laying concrete slab for temple portion and laying tiles to the floor and replacing old doors.

2. Providing sheet cover on the roof of the Garbha gudi.

3. Laying interlock to the flooring around building for easy and safe movement of god with kalasa.

4. Marking of temple area boundaries and construction of compound wall or fixing fancing for protecting the temple premices.

5. Laying ofconcrete infront ofthe temple and covering with Tata sheets, shelter for the convience of the devotee infront of temple.

6. Construction of office room cum bed room with bath room facility for the poojari to stay and office purpose
